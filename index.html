Field is a quiet habit tracker for men committed to the slow, daily work of building the lives they want. No streaks that shame you. No notifications that nag you. No data that leaves your phone. Just a clean, private record of the small things you're doing every day to become someone you respect.

Built for iPhone and iPad. Made by hand for iOS 26.

What's inside

  • A daily ledger Mark each day, build each streak, leave a record of the work.
  • Week, month, and year views See your progress at every scale, from this morning to this year.
  • Quiet reminders Set a time. Get a gentle nudge. No nagging, no escalation.
  • Home and lock screen widgets Glance at your day without opening the app.
  • Private by design Your data lives on your device. We never see it. We never could.
